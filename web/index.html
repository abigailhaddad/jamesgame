HOW TO PLAY

THE STORY

Bill Cipher has opened a rift above Gravity Falls! Monsters are pouring through. Pick your character and defend the town's buildings — survive as many waves as you can!

CONTROLS

  • Arrow Keys or D-pad — Move Dipper around
  • ESC — Pause the game
  • + Button — Open the build menu
  • Walk into materials to pick them up
  • Walk into enemies to attack them

GAMEPLAY

The game has two phases that repeat:

  • Collect Phase — Gather materials (wood, metal, crystals, journals) scattered around the map. Use them to build defenses!
  • Wave Phase — Monsters attack! Fight them off before they destroy the buildings!

BUILDINGS

You're protecting three locations:

  • Water Tower — on the left (you can spot it by the red muffin graffiti!)
  • Mystery Shack — in the middle
  • Greasy's Diner — on the right

Each building has a health bar (red/green) and a defense bar (blue). If all three are destroyed, it's game over!

ENEMIES

  • Eye-Bats — Flying bat monsters, fast but weak
  • Gnomes — Blue little gnomes with red hats, hit hard in groups
  • Henchmanians — Bill's tiny triangle minions, appear wave 3+
  • Gideon — Gideon in his blue suit, tough and tanky, appears wave 5+
  • Golf People — Round blue creatures with different hats: villagers, miners, pirates, and knights!

MINI-BOSSES ★

Starting at wave 5, one of Bill's Henchmaniacs crashes into each wave. They're tough — watch out!

  • 8 Ball — Big grey sphere with a giant toothy grin
  • Pyronica — Tall pink flame with a single glowing eye
  • Teeth — A giant bouncing mouth (seriously, just a mouth)

Mini-bosses have a ★ name tag above them and always drop good loot when defeated!

CHARACTER SELECT

Choose your character before each game! Each one has different stats and a special ability you can use in battle. Check the card to see their Speed, HP, Damage, and what their ⚡ ability does. Whoever you pick can't be recruited as an ally later, so choose wisely.

BUILDING DEFENSES

Press the + button to open the build menu. Stand near a building and spend your materials to:

  • Build barricades, walls, and shields (adds defense)
  • Repair damaged buildings (restores health)
  • Recruit allies — Mabel, Grunkle Stan, Wendy, Soos, Ford, and McGucket will fight alongside you!

WEAPONS

Craft weapons from the build menu to power up your attacks:

  • Memory Gun — Stuns enemies briefly
  • Wendy's Axe — Big damage and knockback
  • Grappling Hook — Long range, pulls enemies closer

SPECIAL WAVES

  • Treasure Wave — No enemies! Grab as many materials as you can
  • Swarm Wave — Double gnomes, all at half HP — overwhelming!
  • Bill Cipher — Every 10th wave, Bill himself shows up. He's a BOSS

DIFFICULTY

  • Easy — More time to collect, tougher buildings, fewer enemies
  • Medium — The standard challenge
  • Hard — Less time, weaker buildings, more and stronger enemies

TIPS

  • Don't forget to build defenses during the collect phase!
  • Allies are really helpful — recruit them early
  • Craft a weapon as soon as you can — it makes a big difference
  • Mini-bosses drop metal and crystals — always worth fighting!
  • You can save your game from the pause menu
  • Journals are rare but needed for the most powerful upgrades
  • Soos heals buildings, McGucket builds turrets — great support allies!
